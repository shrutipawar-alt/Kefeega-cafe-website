# ☕ Kafeega Cafe Website

A modern and responsive cafe website designed for Kafeega Cafe. The website provides information about the cafe, menu, customer reviews, opening hours, location, and contact details.

## 🚀 Features

- Responsive Navigation Bar
- Hero Section with Background Image
- About Us Section
- Menu Section
- Customer Reviews with Star Ratings
- Opening Hours
- Why Choose Us
- Statistics Section
- Instagram Integration
- Contact Form
- Google Maps Location
- Back to Top Button
- Modern Hover Effects
- Mobile Friendly Design

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript

## 📸 Sections

### Home
Welcome section with cafe branding and call-to-action button.

### About
Information about Kafeega Cafe and its mission.

### Menu
Display of food and beverage offerings.

### Reviews
Customer testimonials with star ratings.

### Opening Hours
Business operating schedule.

### Why Choose Us
Highlights of cafe services and quality.

### Statistics
Customer and service achievements.

### Contact
Contact form, social media links, and Google Maps integration.
